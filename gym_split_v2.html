😴

REST & RECOVER

Muscles are not built in the gym — they are built during recovery. This day is just as important as any training day.

💤Sleep 8-10 hours — growth hormone peaks during deep sleep, this is when muscle is actually built
🚶Light 20-30 min walk improves blood flow and reduces soreness without stressing muscles
🧘Stretch or foam roll — focus on legs, back, and shoulders from the week's work
🍗Eat your full meals — recovery requires protein and carbs, do not skip on rest days
💊Take your magnesium tonight — dramatically improves sleep quality and muscle recovery
📋Meal prep for the week — cook rice, chicken, oats in bulk for the next 5 school days
12-WEEK PLAN
How to level up each month
Phase 01
Foundation
Weeks 1 — 4
Learn every movement. Focus on form over weight. Use lighter loads. Master the machine settings. Build the habit of showing up.
Phase 02
Building
Weeks 5 — 8
Add 5 lbs every 1-2 weeks. Push to near-failure on the last set. Increase cardio intensity. Body composition starts visibly changing.
Phase 03
Push
Weeks 9 — 12
Introduce drop sets and supersets. Max cardio 20 min. Track strength PRs. Significant changes in muscle definition and fat loss.
